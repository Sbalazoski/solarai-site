// The Problem

Your data center is ready. The grid isn't.

AI infrastructure demand is doubling every two years. But the power grid — and the bureaucracy around it — was built for a different era. Developers with capital, sites, and customers are stuck waiting for power that won't arrive for years.

The hyperscalers have armies of lawyers and lobbyists. Tier 2 and Tier 3 operators building 5MW to 100MW facilities are navigating this alone. That's where projects die.

SolarAI was built for exactly this gap. We know the Illinois regulatory environment, the PJM interconnection process, and how to structure power agreements that actually close.

  • ⏳
    Interconnection Queues That Take Years
    Standard FERC queue submissions mean 5–7 year waits in congested markets. Your project can't wait that long.
  • 🗺️
    No Visibility Into Grid Capacity
    Utility capacity maps are outdated, opaque, and change constantly. You can't make site decisions without real data.
  • 📋
    Regulatory Complexity at Every Turn
    Illinois CEJA, ComEd tariffs, ICC filings, PJM rules — each layer adds time and cost without the right guide.
  • 💸
    Expensive Consultants, Slow Results
    Traditional energy consultants charge retainers for months of work. SolarAI delivers AI-accelerated analysis in days.
